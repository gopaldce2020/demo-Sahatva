About Sahatva

A safe space where people come together to find and provide support, breaking the stigma around mental health.

Our Roots

Inspired by the tradition of gathering under the Peepal tree to exchange wisdom and seek solace, Sahatva blends age-old values with modern techniques to offer a collective approach to mental well-being.

Sahatva was born from a shared vision among mental health advocates and psychologists who recognized a critical gap in today’s fast-paced world. We often struggle to find the space for emotional and mental support, and many hesitate to seek help, fearing judgment or isolation.

Support Groups: A Modern Solution

To address the need for connection, Sahatva introduces Support Groups — safe, non-judgmental spaces curated and run by certified professionals. These groups help individuals share, heal, and grow together through evidence-based practices.

Whether you're just beginning your mental health journey or continuing one, these spaces are here for you — just like the community gatherings of the past.

Accessible & Stigma-Free

Sahatva is an online platform that brings mental health support directly to your fingertips. No matter where you are, our virtual support groups are just a few clicks away.

We believe that mental health support should be accessible, judgment-free, and always within reach.

You are Seen, Heard, and Supported

At Sahatva, we strive to create a future where no one feels alone in their struggles. We’re here to walk with you — every step of the way.
